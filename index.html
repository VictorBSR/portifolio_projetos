AI and Automation Projects

AI-Powered YouTube Sentiment Analysis

This project aims to help business owners and marketing teams understand how a product or service is being received in the media in a simple and automated fashion, by searching for a specific term on Youtube, returning a list of videos, filtering out the relevant ones and analyzing their comments. Through the use of AI and NLP, the comments obtained are classified as positive, neutral, or negative. Additionally, word clouds and summaries are generated to highlight key sentiments and trends for each video and the entire group.

    Tools and libs used:

  • Python, Pandas, Numpy, requests, nltk
  • APIs: Youtube and OpenAI
  • LLM: ChatGPT
  • App deployment: Streamlit Cloud
